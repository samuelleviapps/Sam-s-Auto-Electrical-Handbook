Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Sam's Auto Electrical Handbook ("we", "our", or "the App") handles your information when you use our mobile application.

Summary: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

Information We Don't Collect

Sam's Auto Electrical Handbook is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your usage or behavior
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Store data on external servers
  • Share any information with third parties
  • Display advertisements

Data Storage

All data you create in the App—including notes, parts lists, photos, and settings—is stored locally on your device. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share it.

If you use features that store credentials or wallet-related information, such as saved passwords or wallet contents, that information is stored locally and encrypted on your device. Access to this information may be further protected using biometric authentication (for example, Face ID, Touch ID, or your device's biometric equivalent) when you enable the App’s BioLock feature.

Device Permissions

The App may request the following permissions to provide its features:

  • Camera: To take photos for parts and notes. Photos are stored locally on your device.
  • Photo Library: To attach existing photos to your notes and parts. We only access photos you select.
  • Location: To optionally auto-fill ambient temperature for diagnostics. Location data is not stored or transmitted.
  • Network Access: Used solely for the network diagnostic ping tool. No personal data is transmitted.
  • File Storage: To import and export your data as files you control.

All permissions are optional and the App will function with limited features if permissions are denied.

Export & Sharing

When you export or share notes and parts:

  • You control what data is shared and with whom
  • Exported files are created locally on your device
  • Sharing uses your device's built-in share functionality
  • We have no access to exported or shared data

Data Security

Your data is protected by your device's built-in security features, including:

  • Device encryption
  • Passcode/biometric protection
  • App sandboxing

In addition, where the App stores sensitive data such as passwords or wallet contents, this data is encrypted at rest on your device. If you enable BioLock, the App will require biometric authentication (or your device passcode, depending on your device settings) before granting access to protected areas of the App.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
